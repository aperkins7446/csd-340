Professional Experience

Specialty Retail Management
I spent a decade in specialty retail management for a certain popular video game chain store. There were times when it was so much fun; I truly had a blast getting to spend my days geeking out with customers over games, my stores always had the best mid-night release parties, and I thought I was going to spend my entire adult life with that company. Then came what I'll refer to as the dark times for the company where the sales of subscriptions and pre-orders became more important than helping a customer find something that would make them happy immediately, and creating lasting bonds.
Paralegal Specializing in Personal Injury Litigation
I was a paralegal who specialized in personal injury litigation. That was a huge learning experience! I was taken on by a law firm fresh after graduation, and I had no idea how much I didn't know. I had some exceptional supervisors who were so happy to show me the ropes, and they made a job really fulfilling. It was always a great day at the office when we won for our client, and knew we had done everything possible to make them whole.
I.T.: School Information Systems
This takes us to my current job: I am a Systems Operation Technician supporting forty plus schools, responsible for our district's state reporting. I support our School Information System by helping with the roll out of new processes, support for our end users, and ensuring the information in our S.I.S. is accurately reported to the state, which has a huge impact on our funding. Most days, I really enjoy what I do, but I always enjoy the people I work with. My aspiration is to be the first Operations Team to transition to a Developer role!
